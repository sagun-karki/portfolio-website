Data Collection & Privacy Disclosure

Effective Date: May 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy outlines how your data is handled when you visit this personal portfolio website. Respect for visitor privacy is a fundamental standard of this project.

2. Google Analytics 4 (GA4)

This website uses Google Analytics 4 (GA4) to collect anonymous telemetry on site interactions, page views, and event engagement (such as outbound clicks to projects or socials). This data helps monitor performance and traffic.

* GA4 automatically processes anonymized IP addresses and device characteristics. No personally identifiable information (PII) is stored or requested.

3. Local Storage

This site uses browser localStorage solely to remember your theme selection (Dark Mode or Light Mode) between sessions. This is stored locally on your device and is never sent to any server.

This website links to external platforms such as LinkedIn, GitHub, and academic publishers. Once you navigate away, the privacy policies of those external domains will apply.
